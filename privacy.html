NRI Money Guide ("we", "us", "our") operates nrimoneyguide.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about visitors to our site. By using this site, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

Automatically collected information: When you visit our site, our hosting provider and analytics tools may automatically record your IP address, browser type, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, and time spent on each page. This information is collected in aggregate and is not linked to a personally identifiable profile.

Information you provide: If you contact us via the contact form or email, we collect the name and email address you provide, along with the content of your message. We do not require registration or account creation to use this site.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Monitor for security threats or abuse

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for their marketing purposes.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our site uses cookies — small text files stored in your browser — and similar tracking technologies. We use these for:

  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics (or similar) to understand visitor behaviour in aggregate. This uses cookies to track sessions and pageviews. The data is anonymised before we see it.
  • Advertising: Google AdSense places cookies to serve relevant advertisements. These cookies may track your browsing activity across sites to show you personalised ads.
  • Functionality: Some cookies are strictly necessary for the site to function (e.g., remembering your preference if you dismiss a notice).

You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may affect your experience on this site.

This site participates in the Google AdSense programme. Google uses cookies, including the DoubleClick cookie, to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites. You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google's Ad Settings or by visiting aboutads.info.

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this and/or other sites. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.
